About Extreme Window Film


At Extreme Window Film, we're in the business of making homes and commercial spaces more comfortable, plain and simple. Since launching in 2021, we've been helping property owners across Greenville and the Upstate deal with heat, glare, UV damage, and privacy concerns through professional window film installation.

The thing about window film is that it seems almost too simple to be effective. But when it's the right product installed correctly, the difference is noticeable within hours. We've had customers call us the same day their film was installed, surprised at how much cooler their home feels or how much easier it is to watch TV without glare.

Our team doesn't take shortcuts. We use quality films from established manufacturers, products with real warranties and performance data behind them. And we take installation seriously because even premium film won't perform well if it's applied poorly. That means proper surface preparation, careful application, and attention to details like edges and corners that some installers rush through.

We serve residential and commercial clients throughout Greenville County and the surrounding Upstate region, including Greer, Simpsonville, Mauldin, and nearby communities. Every project gets the same level of care, whether we're filming a single bedroom window or an entire office building. That consistency is something we're proud of.

Our Commitment

We believe window film installation should be straightforward and stress-free for customers. That means showing up on time, protecting your property during the installation process, working efficiently, and cleaning up thoroughly when we're done. It also means being honest about what window film can and can't do for your specific situation.

Some installers overpromise results or recommend films that aren't ideal for a particular application. We take a different approach. We assess your windows, listen to your concerns, and recommend solutions based on what will actually work best. If film isn't the right answer for your situation, we'll tell you that too.

The relationships we've built with customers in the Greenville area matter to us. Many of our projects come from referrals, which tells us we're doing something right. We plan to keep earning that trust by maintaining the same standards we started with: quality materials, skilled installation, fair pricing, and reliable service.
